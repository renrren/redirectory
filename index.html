Construction Safety Standards

Introduction

Construction safety standards are essential for ensuring the well-being of workers and the general public. These standards minimize workplace hazards and ensure safe operations at construction sites.

Key Points

  • Compliance with local and international safety regulations.
  • Regular inspections to identify and mitigate hazards.
  • Mandatory safety training programs for all workers.
  • Provision of necessary personal protective equipment (PPE).
